startups.scot

A place for startup founders and aspiring scale entrpreneurs around Scotland to gather.

Peer support, discussions and meetups - we're considering what will help the most.

We are working to make an in-person place to gather where like-minded people can share their thoughts or questions.
A place designed to help us all succeed better. Celebrate the highs and carry each other through the lows.

The group usually meets on a Wednesday.
From 9-4 you will find some of us in one of the open spaces at the Edinburgh Futures Institute.

On the West end of the 3rd floor, dark green furniture comprising high table, low tables and a meeting pod.
The map above shows the location in red with the building entrances in green.

We are also talking to the University of Edinburgh for a more official location!

We are also developing a community online - join the discussion on Discord
