startups.scot

What is this? Whatever we make it!

A place for startup founders and aspiring scale entrpreneurs around Scotland to gather.

Peer support, discussions and meetups - we're considering what will help the most.

We are working to make an in-person place to gather where like-minded people can share their thoughts or questions. A place designed to help us all succeed better. Celebrate the highs and carry each other through the lows.

We are currently talking to the University of Edinburgh about our first location!

In the meantime we exist online - join the discussion on Discord
